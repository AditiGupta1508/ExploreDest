Redefining Student Travel

We believe that exploring the world shouldn't require emptying your bank account.

Our Mission

ExploreDest was born out of frustration. As students, we wanted to travel, but every tourism website only showed luxury hotels and expensive flights. We built this platform with a custom algorithm that calculates realistic transport, highlights affordable local gems, and ensures you know exactly what you can afford before you pack your bags.

💡

Data-Driven

We use real-time API integrations for weather and geocoding to give you the most accurate trip planning experience possible.

🌍

Sustainable

By promoting local buses, sleeper trains, and hidden gems, we encourage sustainable tourism away from overcrowded tourist traps.
